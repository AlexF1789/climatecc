Cookie Policy

The Climate Change Challenge website uses cookies for it correct working; you can't disable them.
Anyway don't worry: we don't use cookie to track you or the navigation on the website but only for settings, the cookies used are:

  • opendyslexic to keep the OpenDyslexic font turned on if you did once during the website; it's self deleted after one day;
  • closeCookies to keep the Cookies Policy banner closed; it's self deleted after a week.

We hope you trust us; anyway you can check it by yourself by opening the browser console (F12 key on the keyboard) and writing document.cookie.
