Institut Europa

The secondary school Ins-Europa, called IES Joan Miró before, is located in Bellvitge, Hospitalet del Llobregat, Barcelona. The main aim of the school is to help students to get the Certificate of Secondary studies and prepare them to go to Vocational studies or University. Unfortunately we have to recognize that not all of them succeed and can follow university academic studies. It is a small school with 302 students aged from 12 to 18 years old and 35 teachers. Most of our students are immigrants from South America, Romania, Pakistan, India, etc. whose families have a poor background. Tutorials are very important at the school because students' families can’t always take care of their kids as they should and don’t control if they study or do their homework. Taking into account of the characteristics of our students, we think that this kind of European project offers them the opportunity of expanding their knowledge about other European countries and improving their English. Students learn only one foreign language – English, 3 hours per week. Some years ago, they had the opportunity to learn French as a second foreign language but because of the crisis and limited financial school resources, French language courses were omitted from the curriculum. In the school classrooms there are smart boards and digital books in some subjects. Students use the Moodle Platform in all the subjects’ classes.
